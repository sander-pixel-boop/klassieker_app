## 2025-02-20 - [Streamlit UX Polish]
**Learning:** Adding native Streamlit parameters like `placeholder` and `help` significantly improves form clarity without needing complex custom CSS or HTML. Tooltips via the `help` attribute are an excellent, accessible way to explain the consequences of actions like logging in as a guest.
**Action:** Always look for opportunities to add `placeholder` text to empty inputs and `help` tooltips to buttons or complex form elements to provide inline user guidance in Streamlit applications.
